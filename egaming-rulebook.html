FIFA

Game Settings

  • Mode: 1v1 Kick-Off
  • Format: Knockout (Single Elimination)
  • Time: 6 mins per half (Final: 8 mins per half)
  • Difficulty: World Class
  • Speed: Normal
  • Teams: Any club or national team
  • Same team use: Allowed

Match Rules

  • Injuries and offsides: ON
  • Handball: OFF
  • Weather: Clear
  • Camera: Default
  • Draw handling: Extra Time, then Penalties

Player Rules

  • No mods, macros, or turbo buttons.
  • Unauthorized pause can result in goal penalty or disqualification.
  • Report any technical issue immediately.
  • If not present at due time, walkover will be given after 10 minutes of match start time.

Fair Play

  • No abusive language, time-wasting, or glitches.
  • Cheating leads to instant disqualification.

Finals and Prizes

  • Final: 8 mins per half, then Extra Time and Penalties if draw.
  • 1st Place: Cash plus Winner Certificate
  • 2nd Place: Cash plus Runner-up Certificate

Tekken 8

Game Info

  • Platform: PS5
  • Mode: 1v1 Versus
  • Format: Knockout
  • Match length: Best of 3 rounds
  • Finals: Best of 5 matches
  • Stage: Random
  • Round time: 60 seconds

Match Rules

  • Any unlocked character is allowed.
  • The loser may change character; the winner must stay.
  • No macros, turbo, or modded controllers.
  • Pausing mid-match means round loss, unless both players agree to replay.
  • If not present at due time, walkover will be given after 10 minutes of match start time.

Conduct

  • No abusive behavior, cheating, or intentional delays.
  • Misconduct results in instant disqualification.

Prizes

  • 1st Place: Cash plus Winner Certificate
  • 2nd Place: Cash plus Runner-up Certificate
  • All participants receive Certificates of Participation.

PUBG Mobile

  • Game mode: Classic Mode only
  • Players must be present at event location.
  • Mobile players only; emulators are strictly banned.

Free Fire

Valorant and CS2 (Shared Rulebook)

Match Format

  • Best of 1 (Bo1) for all rounds except Semi-Finals and Finals, which are Best of 3 (Bo3).
  • Map: Random selected by toss

Match Rules

  • If any player disconnects, rejoin is allowed only with proper proof of disconnection.
  • Without proof, match continues with remaining players (for example, 5v4).
  • No unauthorized programs, macros, or scripts allowed.
  • No substitutions allowed.

Match Conduct

  • All players must check in at the fixture time.
  • Players must maintain professional behavior and respect opponents and officials.
  • Disruptions or cheating lead to disqualification.

Voice Chat

  • Third-party communication software is not allowed during the match.
  • Every player must use in-game voice chat.

Equipment

  • Players may bring their own keyboard, mouse, headphones, and mouse pad.
  • Organizer-provided equipment will also be available.

Technical Pauses

  • Allowed only for valid technical issues or emergency situations.
  • Players must call pause through referee or match admin.

Disciplinary Rules

  • Cheating with unauthorized software or exploit use leads to instant disqualification.
  • Unsportsmanlike conduct includes verbal abuse, harassment, or equipment tampering.
  • Penalties may escalate from warning to round loss to match forfeit.
  • Delay of game: Late 5 min = round loss; Late 15 min = match forfeit.

Prizes and Recognition

  • Prizes and awards are announced before tournament start.
  • Winners receive announced prize pool, certificates, and crests.

COD 1v1 Split Screen

Format

  • Mode: 1v1 split screen
  • Format: Knockout brackets
  • Map and mode pool announced by admins before match start
  • First to 30 kills wins.
  • Small map preferred (for example, Rust or Shipment).

Rules

  • Radar always ON.
  • No killstreaks.
  • No bots.
  • No controller mods, macros, or rapid-fire tools.
  • No stream sniping, intentional stalling, or unsportsmanlike behavior.
  • Referee decisions are final for match disputes.

Walkover Policy

  • If not present at due time, walkover will be given after 10 minutes of match start time.

All match operations are supervised by event admins and referees.

COD (Team) - PC

Format

  • Platform: PC
  • Team size: 4v4
  • Bracket type: Single Elimination
  • Standard lobby settings with referee host
  • Match objective: Maximum eliminations in 10 minutes.
  • Small map preferred.

Competitive Rules

  • Radar always ON.
  • No killstreaks.
  • No bots.
  • No hacks, scripts, macros, or unauthorized software.
  • No exploiting map glitches or game bugs.
  • All players must follow admin instructions and match timings.
  • Toxic behavior or harassment can result in match penalties or disqualification.

Walkover and Penalties

  • Team not ready within 10 minutes of fixture time may receive walkover against them.
  • Repeated delays can lead to round or match forfeit.

All match operations are supervised by event admins and referees.
